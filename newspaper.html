What is Health?

Health: is a state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being, not merely the absence of disease. Taking care of your body and mind leads to a better quality of life.

Physical Health Mental Health

Regular exercise, good nutrition, and adequate sleep are the foundations of physical health. Experts recommend at least 30 minutes of movement per day to keep your body strong.

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. Practicing mindfulness, connecting with loved ones, and seeking help when needed are key to emotional balance.
